Welcome to Home Group

Your destination for unique ceramic tiles that redefine living spaces.

With a legacy spanning nearly four decades, we proudly present a premium selection sourced from Spain, China, and Egypt.

  • Immerse yourself in the world of ceramic excellence where each tile tells a story of artistry and sophistication.
  • From the vibrant patterns of our Spanish collection to the exquisite craftsmanship of our Chinese tiles.
  • Timeless elegance of our Egyptian collection, Home Group brings you a perfect blend of heritage and contemporary design.
  • We also offer a wide range of sanitary ware, ensuring complete solutions for your home needs.

Why Choose Home Group?

For almost forty years, we have been a trusted partner for high-quality ceramic tiles globally. Our collections cater to diverse preferences, ensuring your floors and walls become a canvas of elegance. Visit our showroom for a personalized shopping experience and witness the quality and uniqueness that define Home Group.
